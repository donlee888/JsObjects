you are mixing up places where you put tabs and places where you put spaces. Open up your HTML in notepad++. Turn on View | Show Symbol | Show Write Space. Look at your <LI> elements, you will see that you use different characters for your indentation. It probably looks fine on your system, but on mine, where tabs have a different value than on your system, it is not pretty.

Be sure to specify the charset:

<meta content="en-us" http-equiv="Content-Language">

Instead of writing the above in the head element, Write this:

<body lang="en-us">

We have not focused on this, but you should specify the content type and char set in the header:

<meta http-equiv="Content-Type" content="text/html; charset=utf-8"> 

Even better is the following:

<meta charset="utf-8" />
