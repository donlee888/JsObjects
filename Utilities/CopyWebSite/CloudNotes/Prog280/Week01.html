Getting Started

Our job on this first day will be to get an overview of the subjects to be covered in this class. You will have three assignments:

  1. Write an Introduction in the discussion area

  2. Take a short quiz describing your computer setup

  3. Register for some online sites and create an online presence document.

  4. Read through Chapter 1 in JavaScript and jQuery

  5. Read the Introductory materials in PhoneGap Essentials. Read up to, but not including, Chapter 1.
