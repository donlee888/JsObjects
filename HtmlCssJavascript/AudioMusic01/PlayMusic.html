Play Music

This code works in Chrome and Firefox and does not work in IE 9 or smaller or on Safari. You may get warning saying the script has not completed: I'm working on it.

There are two JavaScript files linked into this project. One plays music, the other is utility class which lists the frequencies associated with various keys, and hence helps you choose the key at which the music will play.

On Chrome, FireFox and Safari, the links below will show the JavaScript in your browser, on IE, you will have the option to download the files:
